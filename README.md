## Features

- **Explore & Connect**: Discover like-minded people nearby and expand your network.  
- **Interactive Hub**: Join discussions, share ideas, and collaborate on projects.  
- **Profile & Skills**: Build your profile, showcase skills, and interests.  
- **Map Integration**: Explore creative opportunities around you in real-time.  
- **Community Projects**: Find or create projects with others in your area.
